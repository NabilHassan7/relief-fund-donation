Our Donation Plan

We are trying to make the best effective use of the donations received. The following provides
an outline of the major activities we are prioritising as well as the future plans.

Providing Food Supply

Providing the food to people in the flood affected area. We are also focusing on providing pure and safe drinking water.

Medical Aid

In these times of need providing medical supplies to those in need is our number one priority. We are working further increasing our efforts.

Rehabilitation efforts

Huge areas of Sylhet and Sunamganj have become uninhabitable due to the rising water levels. It is our top priority to relocate them to safer locations.
